How it works

Three steps to send money globally

From fiat deposit to local currency payout — secured by blockchain.

01

Verify Identity

Quick KYC/AML process ensures full regulatory compliance and account security before your first transfer.

02

Fiat Deposit

Deposit USD, GBP, or other currencies via bank transfer or card. On-ramp partner converts to USDT stablecoin.

03

Blockchain Transfer

Funds traverse the blockchain network. Transaction hash generated for full transparency and auditability.

04

Receive in INR

Off-ramp partner (e.g. Unocoin) converts USDT to INR. Recipient receives via UPI or direct bank transfer.
