How it works

Four steps to global transfer

From fiat deposit to local currency payout — every step secured by blockchain technology.

01

Verify Identity

Quick KYC/AML process ensures full regulatory compliance and account security before your first transfer.

02

Fiat Deposit

Deposit USD, GBP, or other fiat currencies. On-ramp partner instantly converts to USDT stablecoin.

03

Blockchain Transfer

Funds traverse the blockchain network. Transaction hash generated for full transparency and audit trail.

04

Receive in INR

Off-ramp partner (Unocoin) converts USDT to INR. Recipient receives via UPI or direct bank transfer.
